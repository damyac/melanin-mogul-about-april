MEET THE FOUNDERS


Melanin Mogul founders Da'Mya Campbell and Shantiuna Sanchez met as sophomores while studying Computer Science at North Carolina A&T State University. The dynamic duo have been inseperable from the first time they collaborated on programming project in Cherry Hall. From winning hack-a-thons to securing full-time jobs, it has always been their mission to push one another to their fullest potential. The mission has now evovled into inspiring others to strive for success.

THE VISION

Our vision is to create a safe web-based community. As seniors in college, we have seen that there is a lack of life guiding resources for black female college students. We want to bridge the gap and provide them with tools that will set them up for success. Though every student’s college experience will differ, we want to share content from our college experience that will serve as guidance and hopefully help them become true Melanin Moguls.


THE MISSION

Our mission is to empower and connect black female college students. We will provide them with tips that will help them matriculate through college. The Melanin Mogul platform will provide blog styled content about life in college and allow users to share their opinions and thoughts on the topics we discuss. Every month the platform will be updated with resources that users can read, watch, and listen to. There will also be a diary feature that will allow each user to personalize the resources we provide and reflect on them to meet personal goals.
